01 · Overview

Built under time pressure

Esc was developed during a Game Jam, requiring the team to turn a creative concept into a functional game within a limited development period.

I contributed to gameplay decisions, enemy behaviour, interface design, visual ideas and implementation, helping connect the game mechanics with its visual identity.

The final experience combines a circuit-board inspired environment, keyboard-based enemy interactions, health and score systems, and a distinctive hand-drawn visual style.
