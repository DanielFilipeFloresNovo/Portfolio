01 · Overview

From events to actions

XD Fusion was developed to reduce the dependency on manual monitoring and repetitive actions in ERP and POS systems.

The solution receives business events, processes them asynchronously and triggers reusable workflows for communication, CRM follow-up and marketing integration.

My work included the event-driven architecture, REST API integration, JWT authentication, Redis-based event processing, periodic detection logic, n8n workflows, automated testing and technical documentation.

02 · Business workflows

Four automations

Two domain events activate four automated CRM behaviours without requiring manual intervention.

customer.registered

Notify the sales manager

The workflow resolves the customer sales zone, retrieves the responsible manager and sends an email with the new customer data.

customer.registered

Welcome the customer

A personalized welcome message is sent by email or, when email is unavailable, through WhatsApp.

customer.inactivity.detected

Detect inactive customers

A periodic process evaluates commercial activity and alerts the responsible manager when a customer exceeds an individually calculated inactivity threshold.

Marketing integration

Synchronize with Brevo

Customers who have accepted marketing communications are created or updated automatically in the Brevo contact database.
