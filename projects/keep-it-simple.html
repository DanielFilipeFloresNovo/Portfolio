01 · Overview

Connecting the factory

KeepItSimple was developed as an integrated computer solution for managing production within a complex industrial facility.

The project connected a remote central database with factory-floor workstations, allowing information to move consistently between different technological layers.

The implementation focused on preserving data integrity, applying complex business rules and ensuring that production information remained reliable throughout the complete operational workflow.
