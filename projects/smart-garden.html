01 · Overview

Managing urban nature

Smart Garden was developed as an integrated IT solution to simplify the management of urban green spaces and their associated resources.

The application supports fleet and equipment management, strategic team planning, garden maintenance and the organization of operational tasks.

The project was structured using Java and object-oriented design principles, with a JavaFX interface, object serialization and a strong automated testing strategy.
