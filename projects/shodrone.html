01 · Overview

Managing a drone fleet

Shodrone was designed as a complete ecosystem for drone fleet management, communication and high-performance simulation.

The project required the implementation of a custom application-level network protocol capable of exchanging structured messages between the different components of the system.

It also included a multithreaded simulation engine, synchronization between parent and child processes, and a domain-specific language for validating drone flight plans before execution.
